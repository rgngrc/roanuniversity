Admission Process

Roan University welcomes aspiring students to be part of our academic community. Follow these steps to apply:

  • Step 1: Fill out the online application form.
  • Step 2: Submit the required documents, including transcripts and recommendation letters.
  • Step 3: Pay the application fee.
  • Step 4: Attend an interview (if required).
  • Step 5: Receive your admission decision.

For inquiries, email us at admissions@roanuniversity.edu
